About Me

Hi, my name is Onalee Estrada.

My name is Onalee Estrada. I am half American, Half-Guatemalan and I LOVE CHACOS!

I have always loved sandals, but my sandals could never keep up with my adventures. They were never sturdy enough. And then on March 1, 2018 I saw them, Chacos, I saw them on my friends feet in all their glory. As I laid my eyes on them, it was almost like a chorus of angels were singing "HALLELUJAH". I knew I had found something special.

Ever since I bought my first pair of chacos on March 5, 2018 I have been hooked. I now own 4 pairs of Chaco sandals and 1 pair of Chaco boots. Some might say it is an obsession but I see it as an investment in my health. I have always needed inserts in my shoes to be comfortable and to help with my back, but ever since I bought Chacos I haven't needed to buy custom orthotics. Chacos have changed my life for the better.
